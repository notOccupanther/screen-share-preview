The Rewind

Why this film, why now

A contextual note for the lead film

A rewind should not be nostalgia. It should be an argument about timing. Why this film now, in this week, in this mood, against this culture? That is the job.

For the preview draft, this section exists to prove the rhythm of the site: hero, then context. The homepage promises a point of view; the rewind page cashes that promise out in a more deliberate register.

In the finished version, this will likely become a short recurring essay paired to a still, a scene, or a single sharp insight that reframes the week’s main pick.
