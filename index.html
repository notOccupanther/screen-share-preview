What is Screen Share?

Not reviews. Not rankings. Not the algorithm’s best guess.

Screen Share is a weekly editorial publication about film and television. Every issue is built around a single theme — a question, a mood, a moment in cinema history — and everything in the issue connects back to it.

We cover what’s worth watching, what’s worth arguing about, and what the industry doesn’t want you to think too hard about. No scores. No affiliate links. Just the conversation worth having.
